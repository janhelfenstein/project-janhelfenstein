## Methodological information

1.  Description of methods used for collection/generation of data:
Data was collected with the help of a google survey found here:

https://forms.gle/BrxfXriGQrFKxGvK8

The survey was also printed out on paper and shown to participants, but all participants filled out the online survey instead.

People in the forest were approached and asked to fill out the survey.

A flyer with a QR code to the survey was hung out to spots at the forest entrance, as well as near student housing complexes near the forest.

3.  Methods for processing the data:

The data was gathered with a Google Forms survey. The processing was done with the help of Google Sheets, R-studio and Quarto. R-scripts were used to perform data tidying, processing and visualization.

6.  Environmental/experimental conditions: 

The survey was conducted outdoors in the forest. People who were in the forest were asked about their opinion. Flyers were hung up at forest entrances and at student housing near the forest.
